Our Story

Founded in 2020, Media Sathi emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize AI-powered communication for businesses of all sizes. We recognized that traditional sales and customer service models were becoming increasingly inefficient and costly.

What started as a small team of AI enthusiasts and business strategists in Kanpur has grown into a leading AI voice technology company serving hundreds of businesses across India.

Our journey has been driven by one core belief: every business deserves access to intelligent, scalable, and cost-effective communication solutions that can operate 24/7 without compromising on quality or human touch.

Today, Media Sathi AI Agent handles over 10,000 calls daily, helping businesses qualify leads, schedule appointments, and engage customers with unprecedented efficiency and accuracy.
